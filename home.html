EduTrack is an innovative online platform designed to streamline the management and presentation of student projects and portfolios. Tailored for both students and educators, Profolio empowers students to showcase their academic and professional achievements through organized portfolios, while allowing educators to track progress, review work, and provide meaningful feedback.

With features like media uploads, milestone tracking, and intuitive project submission tools, Profolio simplifies the project management process, ensuring students can present their best work while staying on top of deadlines. Whether you're a student looking to build a standout portfolio or an educator aiming to guide student success, Profolio is your one-stop solution for project management and portfolio development.
